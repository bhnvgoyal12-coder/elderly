Privacy Policy

Simple Home for Seniors

Last updated: 23 January 2026

Simple Home for Seniors ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data, including favorite apps, language preferences, and app usage information, is stored locally on your device only.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Contacts: To display your starred or favorite contacts for quick calling
  • Phone: To make calls when you tap a contact
  • Camera: To toggle the flashlight feature
  • Microphone: For optional voice search
  • Notification Access: To show notification counts (optional)

Third-Party Services

The App fetches news headlines from public RSS feeds. No personal or identifiable data is sent with these requests.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
